    Sometimes, walking on the runway, posing for various magazines and advertising campaigns is not enough to rise into fame. You should engage in other professions too, and that’s exactly what this model did.

    Aside from runway, she has experienced about another profession, acting. Her acting career involves the blockbuster film like Torre de Babel, Belissima, A Vida Alheia, Passione, Hidden Truth, and Aline.

    Today, we will get to know Isabeli Fontana more. We will discuss her early life, career, her husband and children, Net Worth, and many more. At first, some quick facts.

    Early Life and Education

    Isabeli was raised in an Italian family of Maribel Bergossi and Antonio Carlos Fontana. She spent a happy childhood in Curitiba, Parana, Brazil. Her full name given by her parents is Isabeli Bergossi Fontana.

    In her early childhood, she had grown up with her brothers Harrison and Heric. The diva holds Brazillian nationality and her grandparents are Afemor Bergossi and Maria Izabel.

    Isabeli Fontana Weddings, Husbands, and Children

    Talking about relationship, the model has married thrice up to now. Her first marriage was with Alvaro Jacomossi in 2000. At that time, she gave birth to her first child Zion in 2003. However, the relationship didn’t go as expected, and they were separated after four years in 2004.

    After two unsuccessful marriage, she stayed single for almost six years. Later, she caught an eye to Brazilian composer Di Ferrero and immediately fell in love with him in 2011. Then, the love birds got engaged in September 2014. Soon after that, they got married on the auspicious day of August 10, 2016.

    Her Career in Modeling

    Just like morning shows the day, the diva was keen on modeling at the age of 13 years. She did her first try out in modeling in 1996 for Elite Model Look. Consequently, she moved to the fashion city, Milan, Italy.

    In 1999, she had featured in famous magazines such as Issue, Marie Claire, Elle, Vogue, Numero, Harper’s Bazaar, and Arena. Moreover, she modeled for Sports Illustrated Swimsuit, which helped her to gather a lot of experience in the related field.

    While many models are dying to work for the famous lingerie brand Victoria’s Secret, she entered the company at the age of 16. That created a big controversy in the fashion world as the brand had stated they would not hire model younger than 18.

    After her launch in the Victoria’s Secret, she got a golden chance to work with Versace, Valentino, and Ralph Lauren. In 2000, she got an opportunity to work with Gisele Bundchen, Angela Lindvall, Jula Stegner, Izabel Goulart, and Carmen Cass.
