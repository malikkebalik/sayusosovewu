    Kyla Pratt's Daughter Lyric Looks Just like Mom Dressed as Her Character in 'Proud Family'

    Actress Kyla Pratt's daughter, Lyric pulled off her mom's character, Penny Proud from the animated series, &quot;Proud Family.&quot; Fans were captivated by the striking resemblance Lyric shares with her star mom.

    12 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Actress Kyla Pratt's daughter, Lyric pulled off her mom's character, Penny Proud from the animated series, "Proud Family." Fans were captivated by the striking resemblance Lyric shares with her star mom.

    Halloween's cosplay had Hollywood's Kyla Pratt glamming up her girls for a night of fun. In a series of snapshots shared to Instagram, Pratt showed how her daughters embodied their colorful costumes.

    Pratt's young daughter, Liyah, was all shades of chic, looking pretty as a red-haired princess complete with her shiny crown. Lyric, on the other hand, channeled her mom's long time character, Penny Proud, from the sitcom, "Proud Family."

    Actress Kyla Pratt attends the 2019 "It's a Wonderful Lifetime" holiday party at Los Angeles, California on October 22, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

    Like Penny's, Lyric's raven hair was held in two pigtails as she donned the animated character's signature flared skirt, shirt, and jacket. Lyric's creative look included a pair of black sneakers and Penny's mole on one cheek.

    The photos shared showed Pratt's daughters having the time of their lives posing for the camera and having fun on Halloween night. Lyric's striking resemblance to the star actress was quite arresting as fans were all about her look. Her mom captioned the post:

    "#MyLoves #KirkpatrickCamp #Halloween2020."

    Many admirers were love-struck as they fawned over Pratt's mini-mes while showing their pleasant surprise at how much Lyric stole her face. One fan let Pratt know that Lyric was her "all over again."

    Stars like Keisha Epps and Issa Rae could not get enough of the little girls. Star actress Keke Palmer gushed uncontrollably over Lyric's look while calling her Penny and wishing that she would do live-action.

    Palmer might not have to worry much because Lyric would do justice to a live-action show, as she has been going to work with her mom from day one.

    Once in an interview with MadameNoire, Pratt, who played Crystal Whitmore on "Let's Stay Together," spoke beautifully on motherhood and how she prefers to have the little one with her at work.

    Pratt, who insisted on taking the then little Lyric to movie sets, shared that motherhood puts her on a high. The star mom noted that no joy could compare to seeing a tiny human that belonged to her.

    Pratt made it known that even before motherhood, she was so fascinated by kids and pregnant women that while she was in labor, she enjoyed the pains!

    While motherhood holds a special place in the 34-year-old's life, she has made sure not to neglect her day job. In fact, Pratt once noted in an interview that taking her kids to her showbiz job was a lot of craziness, but she would not trade it for anything.

    Pratt's career has spanned over two decades since childhood, and fans watched her make a transition into adulthood. But beyond the glitz and glamour, the star's ultimate goal is to raise successful kids.
