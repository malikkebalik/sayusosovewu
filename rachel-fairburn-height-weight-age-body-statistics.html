    Rachel Fairburn is an English comedian, television personality, writer, director, and producer who is known for co-hosting the hit comedy podcast All Killa No Filla along with fellow comedian Kiri Pritchard-McLean. The popular podcast was named one of the top 8 true crime podcasts of 2018 by The Independent, Esquire‘s number #2 ‘Best True Crime Podcast’ in 2018, and one of the best podcasts of 2018 by The Guardian. In addition to that, Rachel Fairburn has also worked on a variety of other projects which include Pointless Celebrities, Ghoul Guide with Rachel Fairburn, Richard Herring’s Leicester Square Theatre Podcast, My Favourite Film, Richard Osman’s House of Games, The 30 Most Shocking TV Interviews … Ever!, The Cheap Seats, Alan Davies: As Yet Untitled, Funny from the Fringe, Funny Festival Live, Victoria Wood: The Secret List, Evil Genius with Russell Kane, Stand Up for Live Comedy, When Celebrity Goes Horribly Wrong 2, Breaking the News, and The RDA.
