    Gucci Mane and Keyshia Ka’oir are pregnant!

    Mane and Ka’oir who tied the knot back in 2017 are expecting their first child together. The rapper shared the news through his social media. He shared a picture of his wife flaunting her baby bump in black lingerie.

    Alongside the picture, the rapper wrote:

    “My wife pregnant my life is great”

    The rapper’s wife was displaying her baby bump in the most stunning way possible. She looked absolutely gorgeous with the subtle makeup and the necklace on her neck. Keyshia completed her look with black stockings and garter belts.

    Though the couple revealed they are expecting their first baby together, they did not mention how far is Keyshia on her pregnancy.

    The married life of Mane with his wife

    Mane and Ka’Oir walked down the aisle for their big day on the 17th of October, 2017. They organized a lavish ceremony with the presence of hip-hop royalty and broadcast it on live television.

    The rapper made the proposal very memorable for his wife after he asked her to marry him during the Atlanta Hawks game.

    They have walked through many bumpy roads and always managed to stick together. Keyshia was there for her soulmate during his several jail stints and three months of jail.

    The couple who are having their first baby together has other children from their previous relationship. The rapper has a son who was born in 2007 to his ex.

    Meanwhile, Keyshia has three children from her previous relationship. Though the couple is a public figure, they have managed to keep their children far from the limelight.

    The children managed to skip the camera during the live broadcast marriage ceremony of the couple.

    Talking about her children’s privacy, she once stated:

    “Gucci and I both have children. t’s a situation where I’m proud to be a mother, but I need it to be private. I don’t need the kids in the limelight, don’t want them on social media. I need them to go to school and just be children.”

    They never tend to make the picture of their children public through any media. The couple is happily together with expecting their first baby together. Congratulations to the couple!
