    DJ John Hershock shot in head while leaving club of China Town in Philadelphia

    In the early hours of the morning in Chinatown, Philadelphia, a horrifying incident unfolded leaving a 38-year-old DJ, John Hershock, critically injured. The local community has united in sending prayers and support to the victims family during this trying time.

    15 Sep 2024, 00:00

    In the early hours of the morning in Chinatown, Philadelphia, a horrifying incident unfolded leaving a 38-year-old DJ, John Hershock, critically injured. The local community has united in sending prayers and support to the victim’s family during this trying time.

    The shooting occurred on the 1000 block of Appletree Street, where Hershock sustained a single gunshot wound to the head. Responding officers and medics found him lying on the ground, severely injured and bleeding. The witnesses, who were leaving a nearby club on Arch Street, reported an altercation that escalated into the tragic event. The victim was rushed to Jefferson University Hospital in critical condition.

    The community has described Hershock as a dedicated individual, passionate about music and DJing, and a fervent lover of people. He aspired to make a positive impact through his musical talent, and his friends and family are rallying for his recovery. The incident has sparked shock and concern, prompting the local community to come together in prayer and support for the victim’s family.

    Justin Pross shared,

    “Heartbreaking hearing what happened to my guy John Hershock. Recently he became a Brand Ambassador for Art History and we talked almost everyday. He did everything he promised to do. He was even supposed to be djing our event at Krick Wuder on Saturday…
    I admired John for his ability to be the life of the party, bring tons of people together, his positivity and always offer a helping hand, showed love to everyone & anyone.
    My heart goes out to all John’s family & friends during this tough time. He touched so many lives in a positive way and left a hell of a legacy. Tradgic situation for such a great guy. Till we meet again friend.”

    Law enforcement agencies are actively investigating the shooting, utilizing surveillance cameras in the area to gather crucial evidence. At present, the motive behind the shooting remains undisclosed, urging anyone with information to come forward and assist in the ongoing investigation.

    As the community grapples with the repercussions of this tragic event, they remain hopeful and united, sending their thoughts and prayers to John Hershock and his family, advocating for his recovery and justice.
