    Sarah Lancashire is a British actress best known for her roles in “Coronation Street,” “Last Tango in Halifax,” “Happy Valley,” “Rose and Maloney,” “Where the Heart is,” “Clocking Off,” and “Lark Rise to Candleford.”

    What is the Net Worth of Sarah Lancashire? Salary, Earnings

    As of 2023, Sarah Lancashire’s net worth is estimated to be $20 million. She was reportedly paid £90,000 per year for her role in “Coronation Street.” Lancashire signed a two-year golden handcuffs contract with ITV in July 2000, making her the highest-paid television actress in the United Kingdom. The actress is currently living a high life.

    Where was Sarah Lancashire born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

    Sarah Lancashire was born on October 10, 1964, in Oldham, Lancashire, England, to Geoffrey Lancashire (1933-2004) and Hilda McCormack. Her father is a television scriptwriter best known for his work on Coronation Street as well as situation comedies such as The Cuckoo Waltz, and her mother is a secretary at Granada Television Studios.

    Sarah Lancashire was raised by three brothers named Simon, John, and James Lancashire. She is British in nationality and of English and Irish ancestry, so she is of mixed ethnicity. She will be 59 years old in 2023. Between 1976 and 1981, the actress attended Oldham Hulme Grammar School before enrolling at Lancashire’s Guildhall School of Music and Drama.

    She graduated in 1986, describing her time at the university as “tremendous,” but also “seriously hard work and quite intimidating.” She did not seriously consider pursuing a career in the performing arts until she was 18 years old. In March 2002, Salford University bestowed upon her an Honorary Master of Arts degree.
