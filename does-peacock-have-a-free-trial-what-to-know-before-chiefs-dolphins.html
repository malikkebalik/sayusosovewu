    We’re just a few hours away from the beginning of the NFL’s Super Wild Card Weekend, including a big matchup between the Kansas City Chiefs and the Miami Dolphins that also marks a first for the league.

    It’ll be the first NFL playoff game to air exclusively on a streaming service — that means it won’t be airing on any cable channels. The only place to watch the Chiefs take on the Dolphins is on Peacock. If you intend on watching you’ll need a subscription to Peacock Premium.

    So you might be wondering… does Peacock have a free trial? Read on for everything you need to know about signing up for Peacock before tonight’s game.

    Is There A Peacock Free Trial?

    No, there isn’t. Unfortunately, Peacock does not currently have a free trial.

    Does Instacart+ Come With Peacock?

    Yes! The best way to get Peacock for free is with an Instacart+ subscription (even though it’ll still be costing you money one way or another).

    Instacart+ users get a free annual Peacock Premium subscription with the price of their grocery delivery subscription.

    Instacart+ costs $9.99/month or $9.99/month. Subscribers can take advantage of free delivery on all of their orders over $35, lower service fees, and 5% credit back on eligible pickup orders.

    How to Watch Chiefs vs. Dolphins Wild Card Game on Peacock:

    The easiest (and most cost-effective) way to stream the Kansas City Chiefs vs. Miami Dolphins game is to subscribe to Peacock. Simply open the streaming service’s app on any of Peacock’s supported devices at 8 p.m. ET tonight to tune in.

    How Much Is Peacock Premium?

    Peacock has two different plans to choose from: Premium (with ads) and Premium Plus (ad-free). You’ll be able to watch the exclusive playoff game, plus two more on Sunday, with either plan.

    Peacock Premium is $5.99/month, but there’s a promotion running through January 14 to take 50% off the Premium annual plan — it brings the price down to $29.99 — with the code PEACOCK2024.

    To upgrade to ad-free Premium Plus, it’ll cost $11.99/month or $119.99/year.
