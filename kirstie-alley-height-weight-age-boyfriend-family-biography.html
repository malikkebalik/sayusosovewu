    Kirstie Alley was an American actress who was best known for portraying roles such as Rebecca Howe in Cheers, Jessie Bannister in Madhouse, Ingrid Hoffel in Scream Queens, Maddie Banks in Kirstie, Rose Marie Clericuzio in The Last Don, and Veronica Chase in Veronica’s Closet. She had also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Star Trek II: The Wrath of Khan, Champions, Summer School, Shoot to Kill, Look Who’s Talking, Sibling Rivalry, For Richer or Poorer, Accidental Love, Match Game, Masquerade, North and South, The Hitchhiker, Flesh ‘n’ Blood, Dharma & Greg, While I Was Gone, The King of Queens, The Goldbergs, and many more. During the course of her career, Kirstie had been honored with several awards such as the Golden Globe Award, People’s Choice Award, and Primetime Emmy Award.
