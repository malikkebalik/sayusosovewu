    Maya Devir is a hugely popular social media influencer best known as the creator of One of Those Days. She recently received the award for The Most Creative Content Maker along with her husband.

    Let’s find out the life behind the camera of this really talented woman and what is she earning from her career. Also, stick around to find out who is the lucky man to have her as his wife.

    Beautiful Relationship With Her Husband

    Maya Devir married Yehuda Devir. Both of them have been together for more than a decade. They first met in the year 2010 and Yehuda fell in love in an instant; however, that was not the case with Maya. After being friends for a while, they started dating. Maya and Yehuda Devir walked down the aisle in the month of July in 2016.

    They welcomed their first bundle of joy, Ariel Devir on May 28, 2019. Maya said in a Q&A session on their YouTube channel that finding out pregnancy turned out to be the most beautiful memory of her life.

    The cartoonist duo have also created their child’s Instagram page, with the handle, Ariel_Devir. At the moment, the duo manages their daughter’s Instagram which as 34 posts in total.

    Many of Maya fans consider Yehuda and Maya an ideal and inspiring couple. The couple even gave a TED Talk at Klagenfurt, Austria about The Secrets To a Perfect Relationship.

    Creating a Comic Based On Their Relationship

    Maya became widely popular for co-creating the comic series, One Of Those Days with her husband. In the Ted Talk, they said that the inspiration for creating the comic was the small things that happen in every relationship.

    The idea of the comic first came up when they were looking for an apartment to live in Israel. They made an illustration about finding the apartment and it received a lot of attention from the viewers so they started a whole series about it.

    Income and Earnings

    The 30-year-old artist is doing really well with her comics these days; however, that was not always the case. In the beginning, they used to sell their arts for pennies at the local fairs and markets on a table.

    Social Media Earnings

    The Israel-born artist has about 1.1 million followers on Instagram under the username @maya_devir. She makes about $2,216.25 – $3,693.75 from every sponsored post on Instagram.
