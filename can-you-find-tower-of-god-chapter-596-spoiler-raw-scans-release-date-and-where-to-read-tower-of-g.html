    All About Tower of God

    The South Korean comic series “Tower of God,” also known as a manhwa, debuted as a webtoon. The narrative revolves around Twenty-Fifth Bam, a young boy confined beneath a mysterious tower for most of his life, with Rachel being his sole companion. When Rachel enters the tower, Bam embarks on a resolute quest to reunite with her, opening the door to the tower and commencing an adventure filled with trials. As he ascends the tower, he forges new friendships and allies, creating an enthralling story. “Tower of God” has been officially translated into English by Line Webtoon and has even inspired an anime adaptation, with a second season slated for 2022.
