    Who is Reed Alexander?

    Reed Alexander is an American actor, journalist, and healthy-living advocate. He is well known for his role as Nevel Papperman on Nickelodeon’s iCarly.

    He was nominated for Young Artist Award for Best Performance in a Television Series.

    Alexander began a new job as a Lecturer for Journalism Studies at the University of Miami in 2022.

    How old is Reed Alexander?

    Reed Alexander was born on December 23, 1994, in Boca Raton, Florida, the United States. The name of his father and mother is not revealed.

    He belongs of Caucasian ancestry and he has a sister, Cydney Siskind.

    As for his academics, he attended Pine Crest School and then went to New York University. From the latter, he achieved media studies plus broadcast journalism in 2017.

    Reed Alexander also attended Columbia University – Graduate School of Journalism for his master’s.

    Reed Alexander- Professional Life, Career

    Reed Alexander appeared as a bully in the Disney series Kickin’ It, with actress Olivia Holt. His food blog which began in 2009 turned into a second career as a spokesman for healthy eating habits.

    After that, he was also in 2009’s direct-to-video telefilm Ace Ventura Jr.: Pet Detective.

    Alexander has been a spokesperson for the American Heart Association and Clinton Foundation’s anti-obesity program called Alliance for a Healthier Generation.

    Reed has been involved in promoting Michelle Obama’s Let’s Move! initiative and has been credited with being able to get healthy eating messages to young audiences.

    Moreover, Reed Alexander has published a website on healthy eating geared towards young people and a book, KewlBites: 100 Nutritious, Delicious, and Family-Friendly Dishes.
