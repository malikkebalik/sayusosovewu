    Singer Madonnas Confession About Plastic Surgery After Transformative Grammys Appearance

    Admitting to undergoing plastic surgery, Madonna acknowledged her unrecognizable look at the 2023 Grammys, stating, Look how cute I am now that swelling from surgery has gone down. Lol, alongside a photo of herself in ripped jeans and a Spiritually Hungry hat.

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Admitting to undergoing plastic surgery, Madonna acknowledged her ‘unrecognizable’ look at the 2023 Grammys, stating, “Look how cute I am now that swelling from surgery has gone down. Lol,” alongside a photo of herself in ripped jeans and a ‘Spiritually Hungry’ hat.

    Madonna, who is 64 years old, faced criticism from fans for her transformed appearance, with some commenting on her smooth visage, sculpted cheeks, and plump pout at the Grammys.

    One fan wrote, ‘Madonna looking “refreshed” lol..’, while another expressed shock, stating, ‘So, I am just now watching the #GRAMMYs. Madonna doesn’t quite look like @Madonna to me.’

    Another fan added, ‘Madonna needs to stop with the plastic surgery and embrace the aging process. She’s nearly unrecognizable at this point.’

    Following the criticism, Madonna addressed the issue in a detailed Instagram post, calling out ‘ageism and misogyny’ and attributing the distorted appearance to the ‘closeup’ from a ‘long lens camera’ at the event.

    She condemned the focus on her appearance instead of her impactful words, emphasizing her honor in introducing Kim Petras, the first openly trans person to win a Grammy, and Sam Smith, the first openly non-binary artist to win in the Best Pop Duo/Group Collaboration category.

    Madonna noted her decision to introduce Petras over presenting the coveted Album of the Year prize, expressing her frustration at the attention being placed on her appearance rather than her message.

    She voiced her disappointment, stating, ‘Instead of focusing on what I said in my speech which was about giving thanks for the fearlessness of artists like Sam and Kim- Many people chose to only talk about Close-up photos of me Taken with a long lens camera By a press photographer that Would distort anyone’s face!!’
