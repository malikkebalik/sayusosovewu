    About Kristen Noel Swanson

    Kristen Noel Swanson, an actress with German and Swedish descent, was born on December 19, 1969, in Mission Viejo, California, USA. She gained fame for her portrayal of the titular character in the 1992 movie “Buffy the Vampire Slayer.” Known for her roles in films like “Pretty in Pink” and “Ferris Bueller’s Day Off,” her successful acting career has contributed significantly to her net worth today.

    Kristy Swanson’s net worth is estimated at $5 million as of late-2016. Her acting breakthrough came with “Deadly Friend,” and she also starred in the controversial movie “Flowers in the Attic.” Her wealth is expected to grow as she continues her acting career.

    Early Career and Rise to Stardom

    Swanson showcased her interest in acting at a young age, appearing in several television commercials. She debuted in films with minor roles in “Pretty and Pink” and “Ferris Bueller’s Day Off” in 1986. Her major break came with the movie “Deadly Friend,” where she played Samantha.

    Her career flourished with appearances in TV series like “Knot’s Landing” and lead roles in “Nightingales” and “BL Stryker.” Despite the initial lukewarm reception, she eventually found success with the 1992 film “Buffy the Vampire Slayer.” Consequently, her net worth began to rise significantly due to numerous acting opportunities that came her way.

    In 1995, her role in “Higher Learning” as Kristen Connor, a student exploring her sexuality, was well-received by critics. She also appeared in “The Phantom” and “8 Heads in a Duffel Bag.” Her return to television in “Early Edition,” and her role in the movie “Big Daddy” further solidified her position in the industry, thus elevating her net worth.

    Career and Personal Life

    In 2002, Swanson posed nude for Playboy Magazine and later won the television competition “Skating with Celebrities.” She continued to make guest appearances on various shows and returned to movies after a 10-year hiatus with the film “What If…” Her most recent work includes an appearance on “One Tree Hill.”

    On the personal front, Swanson was engaged to actor Alan Thicke in 1988, and later had a relationship with her “Skating with Celebrities” partner, Lloyd Eisler, with whom she eventually married in 2009 after the birth of their son.
