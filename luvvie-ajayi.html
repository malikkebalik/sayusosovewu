    Biography

    Luvvie Ajayi was born in Nigeria on January 5, 1985. Blogger and author who rose to massive fame for her debut book I’m Judging You: The Do-Better Manual. She saw it debut at #5 on the New York Times’ best-sellers list in September 2016, and has seen it become optioned for a TV series produced by Shonda Rhimes. She and Yvonne Orji have co-hosted the podcast Jesus and Jollof. She earned her bachelor’s degree in psychology from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. She began blogging in 2003, and through her recaps of the show Scandal, her work came to the attention of Shonda Rhimes.
