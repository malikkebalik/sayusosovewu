    Ryu Hyun-kyung is a South Korean actor, voice actor, film director, theatre artist, and music video director whose acting career started in 1996 at age 12 by playing the younger counterpart of the protagonist in the SBS TV series Oxtail Soup. Over the years, she has been cast in a variety of shows and movies like The Servant, A Tiger in Winter, Taxi Driver, Oh My Ghost, The Lover, Doctor Detective, Feel Good to Die, 20th Century Boy and Girl, Tasty Life, The Master of Revenge, Run Towards Tomorrow, Two Weddings and a Funeral, Pray, Empress Ki, Cyrano Agency, Salamander Guru and The Shadows, You Call It Passion, Office, Petty Romance, Midnight Hospital, Terroir, The Artist: Reborn, Sweet Buns, Pharmacist Kim’s Daughters, Perhaps Love, Three Summer Nights, Boy, Mama, Whistle Blower, Intimate Enemies, The Road Called Life, Manshin: Ten Thousand Spirits, Born to Sing, How to Use Guys with Secret Tips, Miss Cherry’s Love Puzzle, Heart Robber, The Divine Weapon, North Korean Guys, My Wife Is a Gangster 2, Marriage Is a Crazy Thing, Truth Game, and City of the Rising Sun.

    Ryu Hyun-kyung Facts

  • Before starting her acting career, she aspired of becoming a rapper.
  • She was cast as Kim Sun-hee, Ga-ran’s housemate, in the romantic comedy-drama television series Sweet Buns in 2004.
  • In 2010, Ryu Hyun-kyung was cast as Ma Kyung-sun in the romantic comedy film Petty Romance.
  • She brought to life the character of Hyang-dan in the 2010 historical romantic drama film The Servant. This role got her nominated at the 47th Grand Bell Awards, 8th Korean Film Awards, and 31st Blue Dragon Film Awards.
  • Ryu Hyun-kyung appeared as Seon-ah, a coffee shop worker, in the 2010 romantic comedy film Cyrano Agency.
  • In 2012, she co-starred as a lesbian woman named Hyo-jin in the romantic comedy film Two Weddings and a Funeral.
  • She played Officer Kang in the 2015 romantic thriller comedy-drama television series Oh My Ghost, starring Park Bo-young, Jo Jung-suk, Lim Ju-hwan, and Kim Seul-gi.
  • As a director, Ryu Hyun-kyung has worked on movies like Heart Robber and Kwang-tae’s Basic Story.
