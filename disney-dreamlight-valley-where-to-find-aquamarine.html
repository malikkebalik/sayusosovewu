    If you’re not hanging out with your residents or fishing in Disney Dreamlight Valley, you may find you’re spending your time mining for various minerals, including Aquamarine. The benefits of doing so offer a lot in terms of money and gifts you can give to your favourite Disney characters. With a variety of mineral deposits across the map – each offering its own rewards – it can become a relaxing grind circling the map and filling your inventory.

    However, certain situations may require you to find a particular gem. This may be for a quest, or even just in the hopes of leveling up the friendship with your favourite character. When this situation occurs, it can be confusing about where exactly you need to go to grab the right deposit. One such gem is the Aquamarine in Disney Dreamlight Valley. Whether this is for a quest or you just want to sell them for a bit of extra cash, here’s where to find the Aquamarine gem in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    Where to find an Aquamarine in Disney Dreamlight Valley

    If you’re struggling to find Aquamarines in Disney Dreamlight Valley, you’ll be happy to know it’s simpler than you possibly realize. Unlike most lucrative items in the game, you will only need to unlock Dazzle Beach or the Forest of Valor – the former of which is accessed very early on during your time in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    All you need to do is mine deposits in these areas, even ones that don’t appear to have gems on the outside of them. You can still run into Aquamarines this way, although it is much rarer than obtaining one that is clearly seen hanging outside of a mineral deposit. You can easily identify them by their aqua colour. If you don’t manage to find one, simply return home and wait a few minutes for the deposits to respawn.

    It’s worth noting you may also run into a Shiny Aquamarine whilst attempting to find one. Instead of selling for 250 coins, like the normal version of the Aquamarine, the shiny variant sells for 1,000 coins in total. If you find these, it’s definitely worth selling them, as if you need an Aquamarine for a quest or gift, the shiny version won’t count.

    If you have an Aquamarine in your possession in Disney Dreamlight Valley, be aware it’s used as part of the quest line for Ursula during a mission known as The Ritual. If you want to avoid aimlessly wandering around to find another one later in the game, we recommend keeping one stored away until later.
