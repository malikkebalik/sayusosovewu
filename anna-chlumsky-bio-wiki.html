    Who is Anna Chlumsky?

    Anna Chlumsky is an American actress known for her childhood role in My girl (1991) and its 1994 sequel My Girl 2.

    She is also popular as Amy Brookheimer in HBO series Veep (2012).

    Age(42 Years), Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Nationality

    Anna was born on 3 December 1980 in Chicago, Illinois, USA.

    Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is mixed(Czech and Croatian).

    Anna Chlumsky is the daughter of Nancy Chlumsky(mother) and Frank Chlumsky Jr.(father).

    Her mother was a singer and actress and her father was a chef and saxophone player.

    Anna Chlumsky has a brother named Frank Chulmsky.

    Her mother used to be Anna’s coach and manager at a young age.

    Education, School/College University

    Anna Chlumsky was educated at Walther Lutheran High School.

    Later she joined the University of Chicago she graduated there in 2002 with a Bachelor’s degree in International Studies.

    Anna Chlumsky: Professional Life, and Career

    Anna Chlumsky was interested in acting and modeling from a young age.

    She began acting at the age of 11.

    Her first role was in My Girl (1991).

    She also did modeling along with her mother in an advertising campaign.

    Anna starred in My Girl 2 (1994) which brought her popularity.

    Anna Chlumsky also acted in Trading Mom (1994) and Gold Diggers: Secret of Bear Mountain. She has also appeared in several television films and series.

    After joining the University of Chicago, she took a break from acting in order to focus on her studies.

    After graduation, Anna Chlumsky started working as a fact-checker for Zagat Survey and worked as an editorial assistant in several imprints.

    She was not satisfied with her work and returned to acting.

    Anna Chlumsky joined Atlantic Acting School for her formal acting classes.

    After that, Anna began her acting career for the second time in 2006.

    Anna appeared as Liz Lemler in the 30 Rock episode The Fighting Irish.

    She also appeared as Mary Calvin in the 17th episode of Law and Order (2007).

    Anna also worked in four episodes of the ABC comedy-drama Cupid and has also appeared in many theatrical acts.

    In 2012, she appeared in David Adjmi’s 3C at the Rattlestick Playwrights Theater, in New York.

    She also appeared in the BBC’s film In The Loop and BBC TV series The Thick of It as Liza, a State Department assistant. She also starred in the HBO series Veep in 2012.

    As an actress, Anna Chlumsky appeared in several TV series between 2007 and 2011.

    Anna Chlumsky was awarded Most Promising Actress in 1991 for her role in My Girl and Best Kiss and Best On-Screen Duo for the same film.
